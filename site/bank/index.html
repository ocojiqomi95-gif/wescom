Banking

Banking Built for You

Wescom Financial combines everyday accounts, lending, and planning services into a member-first banking experience that helps you save more, borrow wisely, and move through financial milestones with clarity.

Checking & Savings

Wescom checking and savings options are designed to keep day-to-day banking simple while giving members room to build stronger habits over time. Everyday Checking focuses on straightforward access with no monthly maintenance fees, convenient digital tools, free access to CO-OP ATMs, and mobile deposit so paychecks and payments stay easy to manage. High-Yield Savings offers a flexible place to build reserves with competitive rates and the peace of NCUA insurance, while Money Market accounts give larger balances an opportunity to earn more without locking funds away. Across account types, savings APY options range from 0.01% to 5.00% depending on the product and relationship structure, helping members choose the right balance between liquidity and earnings.

Everyday Checking

Built for spending, bill pay, and direct deposit with no monthly fees, easy debit access, free ATMs, and digital banking that fits a busy schedule.

High-Yield Savings

A smart home for emergency funds and short-term goals, backed by NCUA insurance and structured to help steady savers earn more over time.

Money Market

Ideal for larger balances that need stronger yields, with access that still feels practical when opportunities or expenses change.

Credit Cards

Wescom credit cards are built to reward members who want flexibility without extra clutter. The Platinum Visa centers on value, with no annual fee and a low variable APR starting around 13.24%, making it a strong option for carrying balances strategically or financing planned purchases. Members who want spending power with ongoing perks can look to the Cash Back Card, which earns 1.5% cash back on all purchases and keeps rewards straightforward instead of category heavy. Travel Rewards options are designed for members who want points on travel and dining while still benefiting from credit union service and more transparent pricing. Together, the lineup balances borrowing costs, rewards, and practical features so members can choose a card that matches how they actually spend.

Platinum Visa

Low variable APR from 13.24%, no annual fee, and a member-friendly structure that works well for planned purchases and balance management.

Cash Back Card

Earn 1.5% cash back on every purchase without tracking rotating categories, making everyday spending more rewarding and easier to manage.

Travel Rewards

Designed for members who spend on flights, hotels, and dining and want points that support personal and business travel goals.

Auto & Personal Loans

When members need financing for transportation, debt consolidation, or life’s larger purchases, Wescom offers lending that emphasizes speed and clarity. New auto loans start from 5.99% APR and used auto loans from 6.49% APR, helping buyers compare monthly payments with confidence before they walk into a dealership. Personal loans from 9.99% APR provide a flexible solution for consolidating higher-rate debt, covering planned expenses, or managing unexpected costs without turning to less predictable forms of credit. Wescom also promotes a 72-hour loan approval guarantee to help members keep major purchases moving. Combined with personalized service and digital support, these loan programs are meant to reduce guesswork and support borrowing decisions that stay aligned with long-term financial health.

New Auto Loans

Starting from 5.99% APR with terms that help members balance vehicle choice, down payment strategy, and long-term affordability.

Used Auto Loans

Starting from 6.49% APR for drivers who want strong value and a clear financing path on dependable pre-owned vehicles.

Personal Loans

Starting from 9.99% APR for debt consolidation, planned purchases, or budget smoothing without hidden complexity.

Home Loans

Wescom home lending is structured to meet members at different points in the housing journey, whether they are buying a first property, refinancing, or looking to make use of existing home equity. Fixed-rate mortgage options such as 30-year and 15-year terms support members who want consistency and predictable monthly payments. Adjustable-rate products, including 5/1 and 7/1 ARM options, can offer flexibility for borrowers whose timeline or financial plans may shift before the first adjustment period. Home Equity Line of Credit programs create access to funds for renovation, tuition, or major expenses, while jumbo loan solutions help members navigate higher-priced California markets. Throughout the process, Wescom’s value comes from pairing loan variety with local guidance so borrowing decisions feel informed rather than rushed.

Fixed-Rate Mortgages

30-year and 15-year choices help members match monthly payment goals with longer-term budgeting confidence and rate stability.

Adjustable-Rate Mortgages

5/1 and 7/1 ARM structures can support members looking for early-rate flexibility before future housing or refinance decisions.

HELOC & Jumbo

Tap home equity for major goals or finance higher-value homes in competitive California markets with personalized guidance.

Wealth Management

Wescom members looking beyond day-to-day banking can access investment and planning services through CUSO Financial Services. That relationship supports a broader financial picture with retirement planning, investment management, IRA strategies, 401(k) rollovers, college savings planning through 529 accounts, and trust services that help protect assets across generations. Wealth management at a credit union level often feels more approachable because it starts with goals and education before moving to product recommendations. Members can align short-term financial decisions with long-term outcomes, whether that means preparing for retirement income, building a legacy plan for family, or organizing assets during a career transition. The result is a planning experience built to connect saving, spending, borrowing, and investing under one clearer strategy.

Investment Services

Guidance through CUSO Financial Services helps members build portfolios aligned with risk comfort, timelines, and income goals.

Retirement Planning

Support for IRAs, 401(k) rollovers, and income strategies gives members a more complete framework for life after work.

Education & Trust Planning

529 college savings and trust services help families prepare for future expenses while protecting what they build.

Insurance

Insurance solutions round out the Wescom experience by helping members protect income, property, and family goals alongside their banking relationships. Coverage options include auto insurance through TruStage, home and renters insurance, life insurance, accidental death and dismemberment coverage, and debt protection designed to support loan obligations during difficult moments. These offerings are valuable because they connect directly to the financial products members already use, reducing the gap between borrowing, ownership, and protection planning. Instead of treating insurance as a separate conversation, Wescom positions it as part of a complete financial wellness strategy. Members can evaluate costs, coverage, and life-stage priorities in one place, making it easier to build resilience while keeping essential protections current and practical.

Auto, Home & Renters

Coverage through trusted partners helps members protect vehicles, homes, and everyday belongings with approachable guidance.

Life & AD&D

Life insurance and AD&D options give families extra peace of mind when planning for the unexpected or protecting dependents.

Debt Protection

An added layer of support for qualifying loan obligations so tough life events do not automatically derail financial progress.
